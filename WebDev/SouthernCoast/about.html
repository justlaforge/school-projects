Southern Coast Cuisine has been serving the South's favorite sea food and southern cuisine since 1959. Our founder, Justin LaForge, is a master chef who uses his own recipes to create the best southern cuisine in the South. He started as just a college kid making small meals in his dorm when he stumbled accross the recipe that changed his life. His own recipe of shrimp and grits got itself known across the whole campus and he was started serving the meal in the diners.

He made everything from breakfast grits to cooking a lobster and clam dinner for the school. His recipes got recognized by the food network and shortly after he opened his own restaraunt in Charleston South Carolina.
